Language arts encompasses the traditional academic disciplines of reading, writing, speaking, and listening that form the foundation of basic education in English-speaking countries.

## Language Arts Education

In American elementary and secondary education, language arts instruction typically includes:

- **Reading** – decoding, comprehension, and literary analysis
- **Writing** – composition, grammar, and rhetorical skills  
- **Speaking** – oral presentation, discussion, and oral interpretation
- **Listening** – comprehension and critical analysis of spoken language

These skills are often integrated rather than taught in isolation, reflecting the interconnected nature of communication.
